Raw Egg vs Boiled Egg

Here's how you tell the difference between a hard-boiled egg and a raw egg: Spin both the eggs and see what you notice.

The first egg is boiled and second on is raw because the raw egg is fluid inside, whereas the hard-boiled egg is solid. When you spin the raw egg, the fluid inside moves around and causes the wobbling.
